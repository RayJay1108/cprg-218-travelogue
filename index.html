My Favourite Hikes So Far

Tent Ridge Horseshoe Trail

Kananaskis, Dead Man's Flat, AB T0L 0B2

This trail was long and hard but very satisfying and breathtaking (literally).

Ptarmigan Cirque

Kananaskis, Peter Lougheed Provincial Park, AB T3Z 0C4

This Trail was so much prettier in snow but very hard cuz of ice. one of the most fun trails tho.

Rummel Lake Trail

Kananaskis, Dead Man's Flat, AB T0L 2H0

Easy trail and highly rewarding. perfect.

The Big Beehive

Lake Louise, Improvement District No. 9, AB T0L 1E0

One of the best hikes so far. A bit busy tho.
